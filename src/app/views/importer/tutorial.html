How to find your session key

Patreon and Fanbox

Seiso uses your session key to collect content from creators you are following.

Your session key is stored in a cookie:

  • For Patreon, your session key cookie is session_id.
  • For Fanbox, your session key cookie is FANBOXSESSID.
  • For Fantia, your session key cookie is _session_id.

First, head to the site you want to import from (Patreon or Fanbox) and make sure you're logged in. Follow the steps below for your browser to find your session key.

Chrome

  • Click the padlock button in the address bar.
  • Go to Cookies > [site] > Cookies
  • Click the cookie for your service and copy the key next to "Content"

Safari

  • Ensure "Show Develop Menu" is enabled in Preferences (⌘,)
  • Open Web Inspector (⌘⌥I)
  • Go to Storage > Cookies
  • Right-click the cookie for your service and click "Copy"

Firefox

  • Open DevTools by pressing F12
  • Go to the Storage tab
  • Go to Cookies > [site]
  • Double click the "Value" for the session key cookie and copy it either by right clicking and pressing "Copy" or by using Ctrl/Cmd-C

For other browsers, please consult browser documentation on how to access stored cookies.
